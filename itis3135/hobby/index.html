About Me

I'm a competitive gamer, and NBA 2K is one of my favorite games to play.

Gaming Setup

My ideal setup is at home with a 120Hz monitor, racecar-style gaming chair, and a PS5 console.

A smaller monitor helps me play better, and a dual monitor setup lets me review the chat while streaming.

Growing up my parents didn’t fully support my gaming hobby so I had to work hard to build this setup myself.

Gameplay Tips

NBA 2K lets you create your MyPlayer based on your preferences.

Perfect timing is key in 2K, so I spend time in the Practice Arena to learn each player’s shot release.

The Jumpshot Creator lets me experiment with different shots, improving scoring and making each game unique.

Why I Play

NBA 2K is more than a game for me. It’s a way to relax, challenge myself, and play with friends.

When things get overwhelming, 2K lets me focus on something positive and gives me a break from everything else.

It’s a balance between fun and self-growth which is why I keep coming back.
