About Us

Welcome to Tattoo Supreme! We create custom tattoos and provide top-quality work in a clean, comfortable space.

Father And Daughter Duo

Tattoo Supreme is proudly run by Milton and Alcenia Purnell, a talented father-daughter team. Milton, with over 20 years of experience, brings expertise in traditional and realistic tattoo styles, while Alcenia adds a modern touch with her creative designs and bold colors. Together, they create a unique and captivating tattoo experience for their clients.
